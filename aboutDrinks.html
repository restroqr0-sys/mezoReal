OUR CRAFTSMANSHIP

At Mezo Jaipur, cocktail creation is treated as a sophisticated craft, using techniques that blend innovation with artisanal dedication:
Clarification: Using advanced clarification methods—like milk washing, we strip away crystals, clear and visually stunning. This process not only refines the drink's appearance but also enhances the smoothness and purity of flavors, providing a silky mouthfeel that is unmatched.
Sous Vide Infusion: To intensify the flavors of our ingredients, we use the sous vide technique—a precise method where ingredients are slow-cooked under vacuum-sealed conditions. This process ensures the maximum extraction of flavors from herbs, spices, and fruits without overcooking them, resulting in rich, well-balanced cocktails with depth and complexity.
Fat Washing: Fatwashing allows us to incorporate the richness of ingredients like butter or coconut oil into our spirits without the heaviness. By infusing spirits with fats, and then freezing and straining them, we achieve a velvety texture and a subtle layering of flavors that elevate our cocktails to new heights.
Custom Cordials and Syrups: We pride ourselves on creating all our syrups, cordials, and brines in-house. These include salt cordials, spiced syrups, and exotic fruit cordials, which add bold and dynamic layers of flavor to every drink. Freshness is guaranteed, with no artificial flavors or additives, ensuring that every cocktail bursts with an authentic and natural taste.
